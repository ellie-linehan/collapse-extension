Privacy Policy for Collapse Extension

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The Collapse extension ("we", "our", or "us") is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Tab Information: We access information about your open tabs to enable the tab grouping and collapsing features.
  • Extension Usage: We may collect anonymous usage data to understand how the extension is being used and to improve its functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the extension's core functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand usage patterns to enhance features

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not store your browsing data on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third-party services. All processing happens locally in your browser.

5. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • tabs: To access and manage your browser tabs
  • tabGroups: To create and manage tab groups
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • favicon: To access website favicons for display purposes

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ellie@3ms.studio.
