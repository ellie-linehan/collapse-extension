Privacy Policy for Collapse Extension

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

1. Data Handling

The Collapse extension ("we", "our", or "us") is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be clear about what we do and don't do with your data:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data.
  • Local Processing: All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any servers.

2. Data Storage

Your tab organization preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Your tab group configurations
  • Extension preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

3. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • tabs: To access and manage your browser tabs
  • tabGroups: To create and manage tab groups
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • favicon: To display website icons in the interface

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. There are no external server communications.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ellie@3ms.studio.
